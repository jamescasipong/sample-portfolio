About Me

I have been passionate about development ever since I started learning C# and Java. My interest grew when I used a system I developed myself. This passion intensified when I became the back-end developer for our capstone project, a role-playing game. The effort I put into this project was incredibly rewarding. During and after the development of the RPG game, I acquired strong problem-solving skills, such as identifying error sources and implementing features like quests, a shop system that involved math, and interactable NPCs. I am currently in my 4th year and focusing on Bootstrap, ReactJS, and PHP. I am also considering learning Lua Script, as I plan to develop a Roblox game with my friends as a side hobby. I am open to learning other web technologies and am eager to explore game, web, and mobile development. In my spare time, I enjoy playing games, watching anime, and reading novels and manga.
